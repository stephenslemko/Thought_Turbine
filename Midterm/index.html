Road

Take to the streets of England. You can lay rubber downtown as you make daring passes around blind city corners. Or marvel at the open world as you drive through the quaint countryside. All while behind the wheel of a car you could only dream of driving! Road racing classes have been expanded to include Street racing and Drifting. Now with over 450 different cars to choose from 97 different manufactorer's and custom builders you're bound to find your favourite ride.

Dirt

If pavement is too mainstream don't fret. The open world is full of dirt tracks, complete with hairpin turns, air catching lips, and plenty of obsticles. Rally racing is integral to the Forza Horizon 4 experience! Cross Country

Who needs roads anyway? A whole class of all-terrain racing is waiting for you. Crash through fences, splash through rivers, knock down anything (or anyone) in your way. Whatever it takes to make it to the finish! Cross country races are a fantastic way to take in the sweeping landscapes of britain. With plenty of custom styled vehicles complete with a lack of doors and plenty of roll bars you'll bound to have fun, even if you end up on the well supported roof!

Locale

Forza Horison 4 is set in Britian, or at least something like Britain. The country side is remeniscient of everywhere from the highlands in Scotland to the busy hubs of Bristol. The designers managed to model exact roads and buildings, yet the world is unique to itself. Get behind the wheel and come explore, you might just find an old classic hiding in a barn waiting to be revived.

Seasons

Watch as the stunning backgrounds change with the seasons. Bask in the orange glow of autumn, as you drift among the fallen leaves. Be prepared for the snow covered roads with the blistering winter. Get ready to grip the pavement in the heat of summer. Can you handle the slick roads of spring? Some race types are only available during particular seasons!

Daily online Events

Everyday new online matches are set up. No need to enter a lobby, online races will have warnings that pop up in game telling you the start time and location of the race. You can choose to take part, or you can traverse the open world looking for barn finds or offline single player races. Difficulty is always scaled and competing cars are choosen based on your current ride to make the race competetive.

Showcase Events

As you complete races and gain scene credits you'll unlock special show case events. In these events you'll gain access to one time only races. What you'll be driving is a mystery before hand, and your opponents may seem unconventional. Have you ever raced a train down a mountain? How about an entire crew of motocross riders through a strip mine? Will you be able to finish first? The only way to know is to buckle up and start racing.
